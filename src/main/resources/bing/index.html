UK Leaving the European Union

Studying at Northumbria following the UK decision to leave the European Union

The United Kingdom will leave the European Union on 29th March 2019.  However, the UK government has confirmed that EU students joining UK universities during the academic year 2019/20 will continue to pay the same fees as UK students and have access to student loan funding.  The funding arrangements will remain in place for the duration of a student’s studies in the UK and will apply to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.   

The Government has confirmed students on exchange in EU programmes will be able to receive funding for the remaining part of the current Erasmus+ programmes until 2020. They have also expressed a desire to be part of the new programme from 2021.

We understand that this is an unsettling time and we will continue to keep you informed of any changes should they be announced. We will be working continually to ensure you have the best experience at Northumbria.
